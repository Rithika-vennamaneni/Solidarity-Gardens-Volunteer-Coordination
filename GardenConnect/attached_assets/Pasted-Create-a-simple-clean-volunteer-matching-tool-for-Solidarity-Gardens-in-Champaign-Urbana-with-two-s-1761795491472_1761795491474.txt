Create a simple, clean volunteer matching tool for Solidarity Gardens in Champaign-Urbana with two sides that automatically match volunteers with gardens.
Real Context:

Organization helps people grow food for their neighbors and communities
Coordinates volunteers across 15+ community gardens in Champaign-Urbana
Need to automatically match volunteer skills/availability with garden needs

Features:

Landing Page

Simple header: "Solidarity Gardens Volunteer Matching"
Brief tagline: "Connect volunteers with community gardens"
Two clear buttons: "I Want to Volunteer" and "Post Garden Needs"


Volunteer Side - Sign-Up Form

Name and email
Skills (checkboxes): Gardening/Planting, Weeding, Harvesting, Tool Maintenance, Event Support, Community Outreach
Available days (checkboxes): Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, Friday, Saturday, Sunday
Time preference (checkboxes): Morning, Afternoon, Evening
Location preference in Champaign-Urbana (text input)
Experience level (dropdown): New/Some Experience/Experienced
Submit → Automatically show matching gardens that need their skills/availability


Garden Side - Post Needs Form

Garden name and contact email
Garden location in Champaign-Urbana
Skills needed (checkboxes): Gardening/Planting, Weeding, Harvesting, Tool Maintenance, Event Support, Community Outreach
Days help needed (checkboxes): Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, Friday, Saturday, Sunday
Time needed (checkboxes): Morning, Afternoon, Evening
Submit → Automatically show matching volunteers with those skills/availability


Matching Results

Show matches based on overlapping skills, days, and times
Display: Name/Garden Name, Location, Matching Skills/Availability, Contact Email
Simple list format, easy to read
"Back to Home" button



Design:

Green and white color palette with earth tones
Clean, minimal layout with lots of white space
Large readable fonts and clear form labels
Mobile-responsive
Simple navigation between volunteer and garden sides

Use HTML, CSS, vanilla JavaScript. Store form data temporarily to enable automatic matching between volunteers and gardens based on skills, days, and times.
